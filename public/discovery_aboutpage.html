About Artist Finds

What it does

Artist Finds helps users discover new music by searching for an artist and viewing similar recommendations based on genre, popularity, mood, and overall similarity.

Why it helps

The app gives listeners a simple way to move beyond the artists they already know while still keeping recommendations connected to their current music taste.

Main Features

  • Search for an artist
  • Filter similar artists
  • Favorite artists for later
  • Navigate easily between pages

  • Search
  • Favorites
